The problem

Why ecommerce brands need dedicated ad creatives

Most ecommerce brands launch their first paid campaigns using product photography straight from their website. Clean white backgrounds. Flat lays. Lifestyle shots from the brand shoot. It looks good on the product page, but it disappears in the feed.

The problem is not quality. The problem is context. A Meta feed is chaotic. People scroll fast. Your product has roughly 1.5 seconds to earn attention, and a product photo on a white background does not do that. It lacks a hook. It lacks a reason to stop. It looks like every other ecommerce ad in the feed.

Dedicated ecommerce ad creatives are designed differently. They lead with a hook that creates curiosity or urgency. They layer in social proof, star ratings, "as seen in" mentions, and customer quotes that build instant trust. They frame the product inside a narrative, not just a catalog layout. And they do all of this while staying true to the brand and looking like something you would be proud to run.

Product photography is what you use on your Shopify store. Ecommerce ad creatives are what you use to get people there. They are two different jobs, and they require two different skill sets.

What converts

Types of ecommerce ad creatives
that convert

Not every product ad creative is the same. Different stages of the funnel, different audiences, and different objectives require different formats. Here are the core ecommerce ad creative types we design for DTC brands every day.

01

Product Hero Shots

Bold, scroll-stopping product imagery with designed overlays, benefit callouts, and visual hierarchy that draws the eye. These are your workhorse ads for cold traffic. They put the product front and center with a clear value proposition layered on top.

02

Comparison Ads

Side-by-side comparisons that position your product against competitors or against the old way of doing things. These product ad creatives convert especially well for brands entering crowded categories where differentiation matters.

03

UGC-Style Statics

Designed to feel organic and user-generated, but with the polish and performance of a professional ad creative. These work well for ecommerce Facebook ads because they blend into the feed and feel like a recommendation from a friend rather than a brand.

04

Social Proof & Review Ads

Customer reviews, star ratings, and trust signals designed into a visually compelling static. For online store ad creatives, social proof is one of the highest-converting angles because it answers the buyer's biggest question: does this actually work?

05

Before & After

Transformation-focused creatives that show the problem state and the product as the solution. Extremely effective for skincare, supplements, food, and any product where the result is visible. These ecommerce ad creatives drive curiosity and click-through.

06

Lifestyle + Product Hybrid

Aspirational imagery combined with product placement and direct-response elements. This format bridges brand building and performance marketing, giving Shopify ad creatives the feel of editorial content with the conversion power of a DTC ecommerce ad.
