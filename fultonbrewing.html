Fulton Brewing

We explored Fulton’s Minneapolis craft beer roots — from the iconic Lonely Blonde to the taproom culture — then created ad concepts that capture the spirit of great beer shared with great people.

The Opportunity

Fulton has built an incredible brand with bold packaging and a loyal taproom community. The creative opportunity is to extend that energy beyond the taproom — into tailgates, rooftops, backyard BBQs, and every summer moment where a Fulton beer belongs. These concepts bring the brand into those real-life moments.

The Concepts

Taproom patio — friends clinking Lonely Blonde cans over burgers, warm summer evening
Product hero — Lonely Blonde can with condensation, golden beer poured beside it, moody lighting
Summer BBQ overhead — Fulton cans scattered across a loaded grill spread, bright daylight
Minneapolis rooftop — friends around a fire pit with Fulton cans, city skyline at golden sunset
