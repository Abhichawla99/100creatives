Sweety Ice Cream

We fell in love with Sweety’s colorful world of mochi ice cream — from Alphonso Mango to Ube Purple Yam — then created ad concepts that bring those playful flavors into real-life moments worth sharing.

The Opportunity

Sweety’s product photography is already vibrant and eye-catching — pastel mochi balls, bright packaging, fun lifestyle shots. The opportunity is to push further into aspirational social moments — pool parties, picnics, dessert boards, summer hangs — showing Sweety as the colorful treat at the center of every gathering. These concepts bring that energy.

The Concepts

Pastel flat-lay — colorful Sweety mochi balls with fresh strawberries, mango, and matcha on marble
Product hero — Sweety packaging stacked artfully showing Azuki, Mango, and Ube flavors
Pool party — Sweety mochi served on a bright tray poolside, tropical summer vibes
Dessert board — Sweety mochi alongside fresh fruit, pocky sticks, and matcha lattes
Summer picnic — friends sharing Sweety mochi on a colorful blanket with flowers and treats

Want to see what we can do together?

These are just starting points. We’d love to build a full creative system for Sweety — from social content to paid ads to lifestyle photography that makes every moment a little sweeter.
