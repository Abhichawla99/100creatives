We explored Airfield Estates’ rich heritage — from the WWII airfield history to the Yakima Valley vineyards — then created ad concepts that capture the warmth of great wine shared in beautiful settings.

The Opportunity

Airfield Estates has an incredible story — aviation heritage, estate-grown wines, and a stunning Yakima Valley venue. The creative opportunity is to bring that warmth and romance into lifestyle moments — golden hour vineyard dinners, intimate wine tastings, summer gatherings on the patio — showing the wine as the centerpiece of unforgettable evenings. These concepts bring that energy.

The Concepts

Golden hour vineyard — elegant wine bottles on a rustic barrel with Yakima Valley hills at sunset
Aviation heritage — wine bottles with vintage propeller and pilot goggles, moody warm lighting
Tasting flight — five glasses of red and white wine on a wooden board with grapes and cheese
Vineyard dinner — candlelit table with string lights and wine bottles, twilight over the vines
