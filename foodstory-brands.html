Spec Creative

We reimagined Fresh Cravings' ad creative.

Six concepts for their chilled salsa and dips line that amplify the brand's fresh-from-the-garden, feel-good snacking identity — built from real product research, not templates.

Our Take

Fresh Cravings has built a powerful position in the refrigerated dips aisle — "Crave Goodness" isn't just a tagline, it's a promise that fresh ingredients and bold flavor don't have to be complicated. The chilled salsa, hummus, and dairy dips lineup gives them enormous range. But the current creative could push harder into aspirational lifestyle territory. These concepts place the product in warm, social, real-life moments — from farmers markets to backyard barbecues — making fresh snacking feel like the most exciting choice on the table.
