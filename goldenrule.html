The approach

Golden Rule Spirits sells more than a cocktail — they sell the feeling of being somewhere beautiful. Every creative was designed to transport: a dock on Lake Como, a rooftop overlooking the Brooklyn Bridge, a sun-drenched beach with a glass of Negroni on the rocks. We paired warm, nostalgic color palettes with bold vintage typography to match the brand's retro-meets-premium identity. The result: product shots that make you want to book a flight and crack open a can.
