ReciMe

We fell in love with ReciMe’s mission to organize the world’s recipes — saving from Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest, smart grocery lists, and meal planning for 10 million cooks. Then we created ad concepts that bring the joy of cooking with ReciMe to life.

The Opportunity

ReciMe has already won the hearts of millions of home cooks with its playful brand and powerful recipe organizing tools. The creative opportunity is to show ReciMe in real kitchen moments — weekend cooking sessions, meal prep Sundays, grocery runs made easy — connecting the app to the warmth and joy of making something delicious. These concepts bring that energy.

The Concepts

Kitchen moment — bright lifestyle scene of cooking with the ReciMe app, colorful ingredients and warm light
Recipe to plate — beautifully plated home-cooked meal with smartphone showing the ReciMe recipe beside it
Weekend cooking — couple preparing a meal together with ReciMe guiding the way, bright cheerful kitchen vibes

Want to see what we can do together?

These are just starting points. We’d love to build a full creative system for ReciMe — from social content to paid ads to lifestyle photography that makes every cook feel inspired.
