The approach

Each creative was designed to lead with the product and a single, punchy headline. We explored different emotional angles — indulgence, health, trust, joy — to show how one SKU can fuel dozens of distinct ad concepts. Bold backgrounds, dynamic yogurt swirls, and real fruit imagery keep the visuals rich and tactile. Every piece is sized and structured for Meta feed placements, with clear CTAs designed to drive action.
