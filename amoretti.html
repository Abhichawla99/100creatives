Amoretti®

We explored Amoretti’s world of artisan flavors, craft purees, and premium syrups — then created a collection of ad concepts that bring these ingredients to life in the kitchens, bars, and bakeries where they shine.

The Opportunity

Amoretti’s product line is stunning — from jewel-toned craft purees to elegant artisan flavor bottles. But the current creative leans heavily on product-on-white catalog shots. There’s a huge opportunity to show these ingredients in action — being drizzled over desserts, mixed into craft cocktails, swirled into smoothie bowls — giving customers a taste of the possibilities. These concepts bring that aspirational energy.

The Concepts

Smoothie bowl flat-lay — Amoretti craft purees with fresh blood oranges, blueberries, and mango
Luxury cocktail bar — Amoretti artisan flavors beside a garnished craft cocktail, moody amber light
Artisan bakery — pastry chef drizzling Amoretti blueberry puree over a fresh golden tart
Product rainbow — six Amoretti craft purees surrounded by matching fresh fruits on white
Italian dessert table — tiramisu, panna cotta with fruit puree, and cannoli in warm golden light
