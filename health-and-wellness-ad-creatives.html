The juggling act we replaced

Most health & wellness CPG brands juggle four vendors to ship a season

A brand agency for the hero campaign. A UGC shop for social. A banner studio for retail and OOH. A freelancer for performance ads. Four contracts, four interpretations of the brand guidelines, four sets of deadlines that never quite line up. By the time the season ships, the launch platform has drifted from the retailer FSI, the creator content feels like a different brand, and the performance ads are fighting their own credibility in the auction.

Health and wellness CPG brands feel this acutely. The retailer category review, the OOH placement, the podcast partner activation, the Meta retargeting flow — the consumer touches all of them in the same week, and the trust story has to hold across every surface. When four vendors are stitching it together, it almost never does.

We replaced the juggling act. One named senior team works your brand under a flat monthly retainer. The hero campaign, the supplement work, the creator brief, the performance ad, the product photography — all under one roof, briefed once, brand-guideline-led every drop. No handoffs. No drift. Compliance built into the operating model.

For health and wellness CPG brands — supplements, functional foods, performance apparel, recovery, clean snacking — this is what scaling creative looks like when the work has to ship across both the shelf and the feed without breaking compliance.
