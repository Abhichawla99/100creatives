We reimagined Manhattan Project Beer Company's ad creative.

Six concepts for Half-Life Hazy IPA that match the brand's science-meets-craft identity — built from real product research, not templates.

Our Take

Manhattan Project has built a brilliant brand identity — the atomic naming convention, monochrome technical illustrations, and science-meets-craft aesthetic create a world that's distinctly theirs. But the current creative leans heavily on the intellectual story without enough lifestyle imagery to drive emotional connection and trial. These concepts place Half-Life in cinematic, aspirational Dallas settings while letting the iconic label art do the talking.
