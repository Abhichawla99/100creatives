We reimagined Taza Chocolate's ad creative.

Six concepts for their stone ground chocolate line that honor the brand's bold, rustic, Direct Trade identity — built from real product research, not templates.

Our Take

Taza has carved out a fiercely original niche — "Perfectly Unrefined" isn't just a tagline, it's a whole philosophy. The stone-ground process, Mexican heritage, and Direct Trade commitment give the brand enormous storytelling depth. But the current creative leans heavily on product-on-white e-comm shots. These concepts bring Taza's chocolate into warm, tactile, lifestyle moments — emphasizing the rustic grit and artisanal craftsmanship that make it unlike anything else on the shelf.
