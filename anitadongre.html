The approach

Anita Dongre's lehengas carry decades of craft — mirror work, zardozi, hand-embroidered silk. The brief was to honor that heritage without letting it feel dated on a feed of fast, performance-driven ads. We leaned into cinematic light, slow movement, and editorial framing: the video opens on texture before revealing the silhouette, and the stills alternate between full-look hero shots and tight detail crops. Built to stop the thumb on Meta, sized to sell on Instagram, paced to convert through a scroll.
