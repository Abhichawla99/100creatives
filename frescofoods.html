Eat Fresco

We explored Eat Fresco’s world of all-natural prepared entrées — chef-inspired international flavors, fresh ingredients, ready in three minutes — then created ad concepts that bring the warmth and convenience of a great meal into everyday moments.

The Opportunity

Eat Fresco has built something special — restaurant-quality meals with clean, all-natural ingredients in a convenient heat-and-eat format. The creative opportunity is to show these meals in real life — busy weeknight dinners, satisfying lunch breaks, meal prep Sundays — proving that convenient doesn’t mean compromising on flavor or quality. These concepts bring that energy.

The Concepts

Rustic table setting — steaming Eat Fresco grilled chicken entrée with fresh vegetables, warm natural light
Meal prep flat-lay — multiple Eat Fresco trays with fresh herbs, tomatoes, and colorful ingredients on marble
Family dinner — warm golden evening light, Eat Fresco meals at the center of a cozy home kitchen table
