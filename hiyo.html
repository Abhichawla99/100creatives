Hiyo

We fell for Hiyo’s mission to rethink how the world drinks — organic social tonics with adaptogens, nootropics, and botanicals. Then we created ad concepts that capture the warmth, the vibe, and the moment of reaching for something better.

The Opportunity

Hiyo’s brand aesthetic is already gorgeous — warm peach tones, gradient cans, real social moments. But there’s room to push further into aspirational lifestyle territory — golden hour gatherings, beach days, intimate dinner parties — showing Hiyo as the drink at the center of every great moment. These concepts lean into that energy.

The Concepts

Rooftop golden hour — friends clinking Hiyo cans, warm amber light and connection
Beach flat-lay — Hiyo cans on a sandy towel with fresh fruit and summer vibes
Pour shot — Hiyo social tonic over ice with bubbles and fresh fruit garnish
Cozy evening — Hiyo cans with charcuterie, candles, and intimate warmth
Backyard dinner party — long table, string lights, guests toasting with Hiyo at golden sunset
