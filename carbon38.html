What We Noticed

Carbon38 leads with performance-meets-fashion — but the current creative leans heavily on studio shots against clean backdrops. There's an untapped opportunity to place the product in aspirational real-world contexts that match the customer's actual lifestyle: the Malibu trail run, the Beverly Hills errand, the gallery opening. The Melt fabric's buttery texture and the leopard print's boldness deserve environments that amplify both the technical quality and the style statement.
