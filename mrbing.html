Spec Creative for

Mr Bing Foods

We dove into Mr Bing’s bold world of East Asian street sauces and chili crisp — then created a collection of ad concepts that put the crunch, the heat, and the flavor front and center in real food moments.

The Opportunity

Mr Bing’s chili crisp is incredible — crunchy, spicy, and endlessly versatile. The current brand presence leans on product shots and bold red packaging. There’s a massive opportunity to show the chili crisp in action — spooned over noodles, drizzled on eggs, starring at a summer cookout — so customers can taste the possibilities before they even open the jar. These concepts bring that energy.

The Concepts

Dan dan noodles — Mr Bing Chili Crisp spooned over steaming noodles, dramatic side lighting
Street food market — sizzling dumplings with Mr Bing Chili Crisp, neon-lit atmosphere
Macro close-up — crunchy chili flakes and glistening oil on a black ceramic spoon
Weekend brunch — chili crisp on avocado toast and fried eggs, warm morning light
Summer cookout hero — grilled corn, burgers, and skewers topped with chili crisp, golden sunset
