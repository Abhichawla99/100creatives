Spec Creative

We reimagined Open Water's ad creative.

Six concepts for their canned water line that amplify the brand's sustainability-first, ocean-positive mission — built from real product research, not templates.

Our Take

Open Water has built a powerful brand around a simple, urgent truth — "More Ocean. Less Plastic." The aluminum packaging, ocean conservation funding, and locally-sourced production create a story that goes far beyond hydration. But the current creative could push harder into aspirational lifestyle territory. These concepts place the product in cinematic ocean, beach, and urban settings that make sustainability feel like the coolest choice in the room — not just the responsible one.
